PROJECT MOTIVATION

The motivation behind this project idea is the everyday problems that my partner encounters with her mother who has living independently at her own home and has dementia. My partner is torn between keeping her mum at home or admitting her to an aged care facility.

Elderly and fragile people living independently at home are vulnerable, they:

  • Lose their house keys, (every day)
  • Don’t understand ‘stranger danger’ and will let anyone into the house
  • Don’t remember who has been to the house that day nor why they visited; and
  • Have difficulty making phone calls to family and friends

The pros of keeping mum at home, is that she will more than likely live longer because she is familiar with her surroundings, the cons, indicated by the health care workers is that if she was admitted to an aged care facility, her health would more than likely deteriorate significantly.
