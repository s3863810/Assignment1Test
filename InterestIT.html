INTEREST IN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

I was inspired to enrol in the Bachelor of Information Technology whilst studying Engineering. Some of the engineering software we used to build and analyse structures were Autocad, Space Gass, Revit, Solidworks, and Civil 3D. During the degree we had to have some basic programming knowledge to develop and run different scenarios. I was amazed what the engineering software could achieve in seconds, compared to human calculations.

I chose RMIT because it is a well-recognised university with employer reputation (8th in Australia) and a world leader in teaching information systems. Growing up in regional Victoria, I was a regular visitor to Melbourne and always remember walking down La Trobe St passing the students coming and going from the building, I would always admire them and think how smart they must be going to University.

I expect to learn as much as possible about information technology, I know little and want to expand on my knowledge. I expect to learn about software programming, computer hardware and how these interact with the community. At the end of this Bachelor degree, I hope to be competent enough in computers to enrol in the Bachelor of Software Engineering.
